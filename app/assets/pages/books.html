RECOMMENDED BOOKS

I believe that by sharing what I enjoy, it is a form of giving Love out to the world. The following lists of books are the ones I read last year and I must admit that they caught my attention enough for me to share them with you. In hopes that these books will influence you the same way they have influenced me, I share my Love with you. All you gotta do... is your own research.

Neuroscience

  • Connectome: How the Brain's Wiring Makes Us Who We Are by Sebastion Seung
  • Blue Mind: The Surprising Science That Shows How Being Near, In, On, or Under Water Can Make You Happier, Healthier, More Connected, and Better at What You Do by Wallace J. Nichols
  • Consciousness and the Social Brain by Michael Graziano

Healing

  • Loving What Is: Four Questions That Can Chang Your Life by Byron Katie
  • The Power of NOW: A Guide to Spiritual Enlightenment by Eckhart Tolle
  • Finding Your Own North Star: Claiming the Life You Were Meant to Live by Martha Beck
  • Quiet Mind, Fearless Heart: The Taoist Path through Stress and Spirituality by Brian Luke Seaward

Creativity

  • How to Think Like Leonardo DaVinci: Seven Steps to Genius Every Day by Michael J. Gelb
  • Discover Your Genius: How to Think Like History's Ten Most Revolutionary Minds by Michael J. Gelb
  • The Creative Habit: Learn It and Use It For Life by Twyla Tharp

Targeting Leadership

  • The Art of Connection: 7 Relationship-Building Skills Every Leader Needs Now by Michael J. Gelb
  • The 7 Secrets of Neuron Leadership: What Top Military Commanders, Neuroscientists, and the Ancient Greeks Teach Us about Inspiring Teams by W. Craig Reed

Interesting Biographies

  • Molecules Of Emotion: The Science Behind Mind-Body Medicine by Candace B. Pert
  • Losing My Virginity: How I Survived, Had Fun, and Made a Fortune Doing Business My Way by Richard Branson
